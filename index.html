I am Lais Borbolato,

PhD Candidate at the University of São Paulo in Brasil.

I have a degree in Astrophysics from the University of São Paulo (USP) and am currently pursuing my PhD there. My research is part of the Galactic Archaeology field, seeking to understand details about the Milky Way’s disk and, from there, reconstruct its history of formation and evolution. In addition to dedicating myself to scientific research, I am a great enthusiast of teaching and outreach projects.
